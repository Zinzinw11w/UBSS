## Technical Specifications

- **Resolution:** Minimum 4K (3840x2160) for hero images, 2K (1920x1080) for others
- **Format:** High-quality JPEG or PNG
- **Color Palette:** Gold (#F5B041), Amber (#E8961A), Deep Blue (#1E293B), White
- **Style:** Professional, elegant, trustworthy, premium
- **Mood:** Sophisticated, confident, exclusive, aspirational
- **Lighting:** Warm, natural, premium
- **Composition:** Clean, balanced, professional

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Hero Images:** Use for main background sections, ensure text readability
2. **Service Images:** Support content, maintain professional aesthetic
3. **Team Images:** High-quality portraits, consistent lighting and style
4. **Lifestyle Images:** Aspirational but not ostentatious, elegant and refined
5. **Abstract Images:** Support concepts without being distracting
